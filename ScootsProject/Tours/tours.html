    • History

    Cozumel was known for being a commercial and ceremonial centre during the period of the Mayans. but the Mayans were the first known people to inhabit Cozumel. They built temples and soon it became a place of pilgrimage for the Mayan people. In 1518 Juan de Grijalva led the a Spanish expedition fo 170 people and discovered Cozumel. Later in 1519 Hernán Cortés used the base for the Spanish during the conquering of the Mayan people. Many years passed and the island became property of the Mexican Goverment. During the Civil War, President Lincoln sought to buy the island to relocate freed American slaves. During the World Wars, the island was used to handle aircraft for the Mexican Government. During the 21st Century ths used for Tourist Attraction.
